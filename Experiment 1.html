About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Sri Krishna College of Technology is committed to providing a world-class learning environment. Established in 1998, the department aims to empower students with technical excellence and research opportunities to meet the challenges of the dynamic tech world. Under the leadership of experienced faculty, including Dr. M. Udhayamoorthi, the department nurtures students to become skilled professionals ready to contribute to society and the industry.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in Computer Science and Engineering, equipping students with the knowledge, skills, and research abilities to meet global challenges and contribute to societal growth.

Mission

  • To provide a stimulating learning environment that encourages innovation and creativity in the field of computer science.
  • To enable students to excel in emerging technologies through hands-on training, research exposure, and industry collaborations.
  • To establish a research-oriented culture that prepares students for advanced studies and professional careers in top-tier companies worldwide.
